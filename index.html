Aquarium Monitor

Keeping your aquarium in check, one drop at a time.

What We Do

We built an automatic aquarium monitor that keeps an eye on your water level and pH using a microcontroller. All the details are shown on an LCD screen, so you’ll know right away if the water level or pH gets too high or too low.

How It Works

The monitor uses sensors connected to a microcontroller to check your aquarium’s water conditions. When something’s off, the LCD screen alerts you so you can jump in and adjust the conditions. It's simple, smart, and designed to keep your fish happy and healthy.
