Almaden Lake Water & pH Monitor

Keeping Almaden Lake healthy and safe, one reading at a time.

What We Do

We built an automatic Almaden Lake water and pH monitoring system that tracks water quality and chemical balance using advanced sensors and microcontrollers. Real-time readings help ensure that Almaden Lake remains a safe and vibrant community resource.

How It Works

The monitor gathers data from sensors placed at key locations around Almaden Lake. When water quality or pH values stray from safe ranges, the system alerts operators immediately. This automated solution helps maintain optimal lake conditions.
